About Our Trust

Driven by compassion, dedicated to change.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities in Bharatpur and beyond by providing access to essential services. We envision a society where every individual has the opportunity to live a healthy, educated, and dignified life.

Founded on the principles of service and integrity, the Shree Murari Lal Dulari Devi Charitable Trust has been committed to creating lasting impact through sustainable and community-focused initiatives since its inception.

What We Have Done

Our core areas of focus for a better tomorrow.

Health & Family Welfare

Organizing health camps, awareness programs, and providing access to basic medical facilities.

Education & Literacy

Supporting underprivileged children with educational materials and remedial classes.

Drinking Water

Ensuring access to clean and safe drinking water through borewells and purification projects.

Environment & Forests

Conducting plantation drives and promoting environmental consciousness.

Women's Empowerment

Fostering self-reliance through skill development and self-help groups (SHGs).

Vocational Training

Providing job-oriented training to youth for sustainable livelihoods.

Aged/Elderly Care

Offering support and care for the elderly to ensure their well-being and dignity.

HIV/AIDS Awareness

Spreading awareness and fighting the stigma associated with HIV/AIDS.
